Leadership Structure

Team leadership roles empower students to develop diverse skills in robotics and business administration. The team is split into Engineering and BAM (Business and Marketing), with engineering being split into Electrical, Mechanical and Programming.

Membership

On average, a full team of Metrobots generally consists of about 70 members, split as follows. Around 20 members to BAM, 30 members to Mechanical, 15 to Electrical, and 5 to Programming.
