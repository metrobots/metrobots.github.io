Team Achievements (abv.)

Team 3324 has won 7 Blue Banners, four of which signify Regional Wins, two of which signify Impact Award (Chairman's Award) placements, and one final one, signifying a Engineering Inspiration Award. The team has also won many smaller awards, such as Gracious Professionalism, and Team Spirit Awards, along with a Dean's List finalist, a Woodie Flowers finalist/Ohio Mentor of the Year.
